GEO/LLM Optimization Playbook: First 90 Days

Published March 12, 2026 · 10 min read

GEO starts with the same fundamentals as SEO but raises the standard for factual consistency and source legibility. In the first 90 days, your goal is not volume. Your goal is trustworthy footprint expansion.

Days 1-30: Source Clarity

Standardize identity and claims across key pages. Align organization details, product names, author signals, and supporting citations. Remove contradictions that dilute model confidence.

Days 31-60: Answer-Ready Content Layer

Publish concise, high-signal pages with direct answers and explicit context. Include clear definitions, decision criteria, and comparison framings that models can summarize accurately.

Days 61-90: Citation Surface Expansion

Earn mentions from trustworthy industry sources. Coordinate thought-leadership placements and data-backed resources to create references beyond your own domain.

GEO performance is probabilistic. Measure trend movement over time, not one-off prompt outcomes.

Core KPI Stack

  • Share of answer presence for priority prompts
  • Rate of brand/entity mention in AI summaries
  • Referral traffic from AI entry points
  • Assisted conversions influenced by AI sessions

Operational Tip

Treat GEO as a layer in your existing SEO system, not a separate channel. The teams that move fastest are the ones integrating technical SEO, editorial quality, and digital PR into one measured loop.
